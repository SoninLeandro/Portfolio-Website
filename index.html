About me

Full-stack developer

My name is Leandro, I am currently enrolled as a student at Codecademy, pursuing a career path to become a Full-stack developer soon.

I am Argentinian, but I have been living in London since August 2021.
I have been actively engaged in multiple projects and I have reached a point where I am prepared to embark on my initial role as a Junior Developer. I am eager to get insights and knowledge from seasoned professionals, as I am fully committed to continuously expand my knowledge and skill set.

I used to work in hospitality for most of my life, almost 10 years in the most prestigious hotels and restaurantes from Argentina, Canada, USA and UK.
This is why I feel communication and dealing with unexpected problems are some of my strongest habilities.

I deeply enjoy travelling and exploring different cultures and traditions, as well as making friends from all over the world,
This is precisely why I believe that pursuing a career as a developer, with the ability to work remotely from any corner of the world, aligns perfectly with my personal aspirations.
